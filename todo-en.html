Success & Time Management

Success Tips

  • The Power of Resilience: Doing great work isn't about daily joy and happiness. It's about loving what you do and having resilience.
  • Growth Through Challenges: People with too high expectations don't have enough resilience. The key to success is resilience.
  • The Value of Effort: There may be someone smarter than me, but there will never be someone who works harder than me.
- Jensen Huang -

Time Management Principles

  • Start Small: If you can't do anything on a given day, do just 5 minutes. Starting is half the battle.
  • Focus on Priorities: Complete the 3 most important tasks first. Everything else comes after.
  • Time as Investment: Every moment of youth is a time investment in yourself. Choose wisely.
